    OryxRating

    The aim of Oryx is to help the users who want to buy any tech gadget by letting them know the value of the product. We here like to improve the concept of rating system by including the real people into it and dividing the ratings into different categories, Users can rate each aspect of the product and the one who wants to buy will be lucky to know the true value of the product. We are not responsible for the ratings provided and all the ratings are given by the users who rate the products to help others by the grace of their own experience with the product. Oryx also wants to help the companies in evaluating their products because the ratings are not given by only some members of the website but their own customers rate them according to their experience with the product. This also helps them to evaluate their stand in the market.
